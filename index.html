Cardio

Cardio exercises in the gym, like running, cycling, and rowing, boost heart health and stamina by increasing your heart rate. These workouts help burn calories, improve endurance, and enhance overall fitness.

Strength Training

Focuses on building muscle mass and strength through exercises like weightlifting, resistance training, and bodyweight exercises.

Flexibility and Mobility

Enhances range of motion and reduces injury risk through stretching, yoga, and Pilates.

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)

Combines short bursts of intense exercise with periods of rest or low-intensity activity for a highly effective workout.

Functional Training

Mimics everyday movements to improve overall functional strength and stability, using exercises like kettlebell swings, medicine ball throws, and balance drills.
